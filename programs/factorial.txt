strout Welcome
in ax ;number which factorial will be calculated
push 1
pop bx ;place where factorial will be calculated

call Factorial
push bx
out
hlt

Factorial:
    push ax
    push bx
    mul
    pop bx

    push ax
    push 1
    sub
    pop ax
    push ax
    push 1
    jbe Iteration
        call Factorial
    Iteration:
        ret

Welcome:
    db "$Please enter number which factorial will be calculated$"
